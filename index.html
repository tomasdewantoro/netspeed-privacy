Privacy Policy for Netspeed

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Netspeed - Internet Speed Test ("the App") handles your data. We take your privacy seriously and aim to be transparent about how we use your information.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage Only: All test results, history, and graph data are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any external servers to collect, store, or share your personal network data.

2. Permissions & Usage

  • Location Permission: Required by Android/iOS to access WiFi network information (SSID/BSSID) and signal strength. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Network State: Used to perform the Speed Test and Ping Test.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use standard networking libraries to perform speed tests. These libraries facilitate the transfer of dummy data to measure bandwidth and do not collect identifiable user information.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information and is safe for use by all ages.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact the developer via the support email listed on the Google Play Store.
