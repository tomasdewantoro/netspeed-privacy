Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the software application Netspeed - Internet Speed Test ("App") for mobile devices. This policy describes how the App handles technical and personal data.

Commitment to Privacy: Netspeed is designed as a "Local-First" utility. We do not maintain any central servers for user data, and we do not track your identity.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App requires specific permissions to function accurately:

  • Location Data: To provide WiFi signal analysis and SSID identification, Android/iOS requires Location Access. We use this only for network identification. We do not track your physical movements or store your GPS coordinates.
  • Network State: We access your network connection status to determine if you are on Cellular or WiFi before initiating tests.

2. Data Storage and Persistence

All test history, including download/upload speeds, latency (ping), and timestamps, is stored exclusively in the App's **local database** on your physical device.

  • We do not upload your test history to any cloud service.
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all stored history data.

3. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security features. We do not transmit your data over the internet to our own servers, which significantly reduces the risk of data breaches.

4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA Compliance)

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, you have full control over your information:

  • Access/Portability: You can view all your data directly within the App's history tab.
  • Deletion: You can clear your history at any time within the App settings or by clearing the App cache in your device settings.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon being posted on this URL.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the App, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via the developer email provided in the Google Play Store listing.
